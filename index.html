WWF's mission is to stop the degradation of our planet's natural environment.

What is Climate Change?

Climate change is the alteration of weather patterns from the norm. This is usually due to the accumulation of greenhouse gasses like CO2 and NO2, usually from burning, industrial effusions and other related human activities.

WWF and climate change.

WWF has actively invested their efforts into dealing with climate change, because how would wildlife survive in an environment whose climate disfavours that purpose. They've done this by sensitizing masses on cleaner waste disposal techniques and the importance of adopting safer fuel management practices.
